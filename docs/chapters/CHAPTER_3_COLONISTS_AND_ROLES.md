## What This Chapter Is

Chapter 3 introduces **actors**.

From this point onward:
- wallets are not just holders
- balances are not just territory
- actions are not just transfers

## Colonists: A Neutral Definition

A **colonist** is any wallet that holds Solum.

Nothing more.
Nothing less.

There is no identity claim.
No off-chain linkage.
No human assumption.

A colonist is defined solely by:
- presence (holding territory)
- time
- activity

---

## Roles Are Not Status

This chapter introduces **roles**, not ranks.

A role:
- describes a behavior
- is derived from verifiable on-chain signals
- can coexist with other roles
- can change over time

Roles are **descriptive**, never prescriptive.

---

## Why Roles Exist

Roles exist to make the system **legible**.

They allow users to:
- understand how territory is being used
- observe patterns of participation
- see diversity of behavior without judgment

Roles are tools for understanding, not incentives.
